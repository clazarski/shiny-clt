Task 2: Confirm the distribution

Use the law of large numbers to confirm or adjust your choice for the parent distributions of samples taken from distributions A,B,C and D. Were you correct?

  1. What sample size did it take to be confident you have identified the shape of each distribution?
  2. Now that you know the parent distribution shape, identify the smallest sample size that you feel confident can be used to identify each distribution.
  3. Do the sample sizes necessary to identify a parent distribution vary for each distribution? Can you explain when a larger sample size is necessary?
